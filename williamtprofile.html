Ideal Jobs

William’s Ideal Job is a Network and Systems Administrator

In this position, critical and creative thinking is a must. William would determine an organisation’s system needs, gather data, and install network hardware and software. He would also upgrade, maintain and repair networks, ensure that systems are operating correctly, maintain network and computer system security, and evaluate and optimize networks. He would monitor system performance and efficiencies, add users to a network, assign and update security permissions on the network, and train users in the correct use of systems. He would solve any errors or issues once alerted to the problem.
