Group Reflection

During this assignment, we were able to complete most of the tasks assigned to us. These tasks include creating a prototype application, producing a pitch and setting the basics of a business plan for Bake Bot. For many of us, we did well with learning new skills, for example, learning how to use “protype.io”, GitHub, and writing code for a website. Building on assignment 2, the communication side has increased a couple of days before the assignment is due.

From the beginning of this assignment and the previous assignment, communication has been one of our weaknesses. Unfortunately, we have not improved. Only in the last couple of days communication has improved significantly. We also need to find a better way to collaborate with this assignment. Maybe to find a better time for us to meet or to have another communication too such as “WhatsApp” or “google classrooms”. As a team, we needed to have a better understanding of the task’s requirement for the assignment. This includes asking questions to classify sections of the assignment.

Everyone manages to complete all task before the check deadline. Another surprising factor was how communication improve once the deadline grew closer.

As a group, many issues will arise when working in a team. One key issue that we need to focus on is to have a better way of communication. If communication breaks, the task will not be complete on time. We also need to better understand the team’s strength and weakness.

Still, I feel that better communication could have gotten the team back together sooner and I wouldn’t have had to intervene as the new team leader. I don’t regret becoming the de facto leader as I have learned many things I wouldn’t have otherwise. But one thing that I did learn that surprised me was that I am not a good team leader. I have definitely learned that it is the team leader’s job to get people moving and that indecisive leadership wastes time and good effort from other teammates. I think our team did well with the limited time we had to get this project done and, individually, members like Peter and William went above and beyond to get the work done fast. However, as a group, we suffered from poor communication, time management, and bad leadership.
